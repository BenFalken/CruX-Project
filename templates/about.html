About the Game

Puffy's Adventure is the first step towards developing a web application to process and refine motor impulses!
We aim to expand the role and power of non-invasive BCIs in education and modern-day consumption, showing how neurotech can eventually be integrated into everyday life.

This project employs the following tools:
- Flask
- Firebase
- Keras

The authors of this project are members of CruX BCI Team 2.

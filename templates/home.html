The Adventures of Puffy

A Mind-Based Video Game!

No EEG required to play! Just press the left & right arrow keys to move Puffy left & right. Collect as much sparkle dust as you can before your time runs out!


Puffy is cute but hardy koala brain, whom you must guide left and right to collect life-sustaining sparkle dust. You can play using either arrow keys or using an OpenBCI headset.
